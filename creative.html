Author

Any classmate from my high school will tell you, that I can write a good story. Inspired by many books I've read, my vivid imagination and infinite creativity, I've written a bunch of stories that were read by the teacher to others, as an example of a good story. Many awards that I've received from best performance in English, possibly stemed from my good ol' literature. For years, I have strayed from this, forgetting the thrill of putting a literal mental universe into words, so beautifully that a reader falls in love. I'm getting back to my younger self, so on a Sunday afternoon, you might find me in a cafe, spending my free time to adventure in my supreme universe, my mind, trying to write down everything I see there. Through Her Tears, is a book still under production. Fingers crossed we get it published.

Through Her Tears

Burdened by a decade of grief over her father’s sudden death and the relentless verbal abuse from her now-alcoholic mother, 22-year-old Steph reaches her breaking point. Convinced she can no longer endure the pain, she runs to what she believes will be the end of her existence. But her 9-year-old sister, Lily, refuses to let her go, following her into the night and standing by her side. What begins as a moment of despair transforms into a raw and powerful journey as the sisters fight to stay alive, for in the dark, is not a safe place to be.

Psychological .Thriller. Crime
